Privacy Policy

App: Fuzhou Mahjong Scorekeeper
Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

Fuzhou Mahjong Scorekeeper is a scoring tool for Fuzhou-style Mahjong games.

Data Collection

We collect no data. The app:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Does not use analytics or tracking
  • Does not share any information with third parties
  • Stores all game data locally on your device only

All scores and game history exist only on your device and are cleared when you reset or uninstall the app.

Contact

Questions? Contact: privacy@leekly.org

This policy applies to Fuzhou Mahjong Scorekeeper on both iOS (Apple App Store) and Android (Google Play Store).
